Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters. We explain clearly what data we handle and why.

Last updated: July 2026. Base template subject to review by your legal advisor.

1. Data controller

Nexora Studio ([CUIT], based in Gualeguay, Entre Ríos, Argentina) is the data controller for the personal data you provide through this site and our contact channels.

2. Data we collect

  • Contact details you voluntarily send us (name, email, phone/WhatsApp, message).
  • Information about the project or inquiry you share with us.
  • Non-identifying browsing technical data (device type, approximate country, visited pages) via first-party or analytics cookies.

3. Purpose

We use your data to answer inquiries, prepare quotes, provide and improve our services, and comply with legal obligations. We do not sell or transfer your data to third parties for commercial purposes.

4. Legal basis

Processing is based on your consent, the pre-contractual/contractual relationship and compliance with legal obligations, under Argentina's Personal Data Protection Law No. 25.326.

5. Your rights (Habeas Data)

At any time you may exercise your rights of access, rectification, update, confidentiality and erasure (ARCO) by writing to contacto@nexorastudio.com. If the response is unsatisfactory, you may contact the Agency for Access to Public Information.

6. Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary for the stated purposes or as required by applicable law, and delete it when no longer needed.

7. Cookies

This site may use cookies to improve the experience and measure traffic. You can configure your browser to reject them; some features may be affected.

8. Contact

For any questions about this policy: contacto@nexorastudio.com.
